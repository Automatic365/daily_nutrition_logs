# Weekly Strength Training Schedule (Locked Reference)

This document defines the **authoritative strength-training structure** to be used going forward. It is designed to integrate with Muay Thai, fasting days, and recovery constraints. Changes should be deliberate and infrequent.

---

## Core Rules (Read First)

1. **One synergy set per muscle group**
   - No volume creep
   - Sessions should last **5–10 minutes total**

2. **Tension > fatigue**
   - Stop at technical failure
   - No pump chasing, no finishers

3. **Legs are never trained before hard Muay Thai**

4. **Wednesday, Thursday, and Sunday are support days**
   - Neural, isometric, or low-fatigue work only

---

## Weekly Schedule

### **Monday — Chest + Triceps**
**Intent:** High-quality stimulus, post-weekend recovery
- Chest: 1 synergy set (press or fly pattern)
- Triceps: 1 synergy set (extension or pressdown pattern)

---

### **Tuesday — Back + Biceps**
**Intent:** Upper-back density and arm maintenance
- Back: 1 synergy set (row or pull pattern)
- Biceps: 1 synergy set (curl or hold)

*Note:* If Muay Thai intensity is very high, pull volume stays tight and conservative.

---

### **Wednesday — Grip, Abs, Calves**
**Intent:** Structural support, not fatigue
- Grip: isometrics or short sets
- Abs: bracing or controlled flexion
- Calves: slow tension or isometric holds

No grinding. Leave feeling better than when you started.

---

### **Thursday — Neck + Hips**
**Intent:** Durability, posture, clinch strength (pre–Muay Thai)
- Neck: controlled flexion/extension or isometrics
- Hips: abduction, extension, or stability work

Neural focus only. No soreness allowed.

---

### **Friday — Legs *or* Shoulders** (Alternate Weekly)
**Intent:** Primary lower-body or shoulder stimulus

**When Legs:**
- Squat or hinge pattern
- 1 hard synergy set

**When Shoulders:**
- Press **or** lateral raise (not both)
- 1 synergy set

This is the **only heavy leg slot of the week**.

---

### **Saturday — Legs *or* Shoulders**
**Intent:** Complete the weekly rotation
- Perform the body part not trained on Friday
- Slightly lighter if fatigue carries over

---

### **Sunday — Grip, Abs, Calves (Support Day)**
**Intent:** Reinforce weak links during fasting / low intake

**Rules:**
- Isometrics only
- Perfect reps
- No metabolic stress
- No pump chasing

Sunday work should feel restorative, not demanding.

---

## Success Criteria

You are running this correctly if:
- Sessions stay under 10 minutes
- No muscle feels chronically sore
- Muay Thai performance is stable or improving
- Waist continues to trend down

You are drifting if:
- Sessions get longer
- You add “just one more” exercise
- Recovery feels muddy

When in doubt: **remove volume, never add it**.
